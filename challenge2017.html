DAVIS Challenge on Video Object Segmentation 2017

Workshop in conjunction with CVPR 2017, Honolulu, Hawaii

The DAVIS Challenge on Video Object Segmentation 2017 will be held as a workshop in CVPR 2017, Honolulu, Hawaii. The competition will be about video object segmentation on the high-quality annotation of DAVIS. We will release an extended set of annotated sequences for the challenge.
